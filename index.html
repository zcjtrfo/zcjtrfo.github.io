About Me

Hello! I’m Rob, an accent coach specialising in Southern British English, sometimes called Received Pronunciation (RP). I work with non-native speakers who want their spoken English to feel effortless and engaging — so their ideas come across exactly as intended.

What We’ll Work On

  • Reducing moments where listeners ask you to repeat yourself
  • Developing a natural rhythm, stress, and intonation
  • Fine-tuning vowel and consonant sounds that are tricky for you
  • Building a style of speech that feels authentic but easily understood by native speakers
